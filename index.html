Our Heritage

At Aurum Venison, our deer are raised in carefully protected estates. Through a rare mineral oil bathing ritual, the animals develop a uniquely refined fat structure that results in extraordinary flavor.

The process is costly, rare, and controversial — but it creates one of the most luxurious meats available anywhere in the world.
